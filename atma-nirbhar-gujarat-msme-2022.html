Atma Nirbhar Gujarat Scheme for Assistance to MSMEs 2022

Empowering Gujarat's industries with financial support and incentives
✅ Key Benefits Under Atmanirbhar Gujarat MSME Scheme (2022-2027)
  1. 🎁 Capital Subsidy (Only for Micro Enterprises)
    Eligibility: New or existing micro manufacturing units undertaking expansion/diversification.

    Subsidy Amount (based on Taluka Category):
    • Category 1: 25% of Term Loan, up to ₹35 lakhs
    • Category 2: 20% of Term Loan, up to ₹30 lakhs
    • Category 3 & Municipal Areas: 10% of Term Loan, up to ₹10 lakhs
    Note: Apply within 1 year of commercial production/first disbursement. Subsidy released after production begins.
  2. 💸 Interest Subsidy (Term Loan)
    Eligibility: New/expanding manufacturing & service sector units.

    Manufacturing Sector:
    • Category 1: 7% subsidy, max ₹35 lakhs/year for 7 years
    • Category 2: 6% subsidy, max ₹30 lakhs/year for 6 years
    • Category 3 & Municipal Areas: 5% subsidy, max ₹25 lakhs/year for 5 years
    Service Sector (on machinery, furniture, etc.): Same rates & limits as above.

    Additional 1% Subsidy for:
    • Women, differently-abled, startups, and entrepreneurs <35 years of age
    Condition: Enterprise must bear minimum 2% interest.
    Bonus: Interest subsidy available for captive solar/renewable power plants.
  3. 💼 Net SGST Reimbursement (Manufacturing MSMEs only)
    • Category 1: 100% SGST reimbursement for 10 years (max 7.5% of FCI/year)
    • Category 2: 90% | Category 3 & Municipal: 80%
    Based on lower of actual GFCI or bank-approved project cost.
  4. 👨‍🏭 EPF Reimbursement
    100% employer EPF contribution reimbursed (up to ₹1,800/month/employee)
    Duration: Valid for 10 years for new employees hired in Gujarat only
  5. 🧾 ERP Software Support
    65% cost covered, up to ₹1,00,000
    For cloud-based or licensed ERP (sales, inventory, HR, accounting)
    Includes installation & service charges (hardware not covered)
  6. 🏢 Assistance for Rent
    65% rent subsidy, up to ₹1 lakh/year
    Duration: Valid for 5 years
  7. ⚡ Electricity Duty Exemption
    100% exemption on electricity duty for 5 years
  8. 🔌 Power Connection Charges Subsidy (Only for MSMEs)
    35% subsidy (max ₹5 lakhs) on power connection cost
    Based on estimate submitted to Gujarat Vij Company
    Example: ₹5 lakh subsidy for 700 kW load (e.g. M/s Singhal Recyclers Pvt. Ltd.)
🎯 Who Can Apply?
  • Registered Micro, Small, or Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in Gujarat
  • Must start commercial production between 5 Oct 2022 – 4 Oct 2027
  • Eligible for either this scheme or previous one (not both)
📈 Why This Scheme Matters
  • ✅ Cuts tax & wage costs
  • ✅ Boosts automation, quality, & ERP adoption
  • ✅ Encourages formal job creation
  • ✅ Offers real financial relief for business expansion
